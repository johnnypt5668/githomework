Creamy Garlic Butter Salmon


Ingredients

4 salmon filets (6 ounces)
1 tbsp olive oil
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 tbsp butter
2 cloves garlic (chopped)
2 tbsp chopped parsley
salt & pepper (to taste)
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Instructions:

1 Season both sides of the salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
2 Over medium-high heat in a skillet, pan-fry the salmon in olive oil until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of your salmon. Drain some of the excess oil from the pan, leaving a little to saute the garlic.
3 Melt butter in the pan, tilt the pan towards you and spoon the butter generously over the salmon fillets.
4 Remove salmon from the pan. Sauté the garlic until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
5 Reduce heat to medium-low and pour in chicken stock and heavy cream.
6 Add Parmesan cheese and stir to melt the cheese into the sauce.  Season with parsley and salt and pepper to taste.
7 Simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens. Add salmon back to the pan and simmer for a few more minutes until the salmon is warmed through.
8 Spoon the sauce over the salmon, top with more parsley then dish and serve hot.
